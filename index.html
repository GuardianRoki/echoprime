What is EchoPrime?

EchoPrime is a build optimization tool for Wuthering Waves players. The game gives you echoes to equip on your characters, but figuring out if your echo stats are actually good is not always straightforward. EchoPrime takes care of that. You input your echo stats, and the site grades your build from S down to F based on how well your substats match what your character actually needs.

Whether you are just getting into endgame or trying to push your best characters to their limit, this site is built to help you spend less time guessing and more time farming the right things.

What you can do here

  • Browse the character roster and see ideal echo builds for each resonator
  • Input your equipped echoes and receive a real time letter grade
  • Look up any echo from the full database and see its stats and set bonuses
  • See which substats are most valuable per character so you know what to keep
  • Find out which echoes to farm and where to get them
